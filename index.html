Enterprise resource planning (ERP) is a cross-functional enterprise system driven by an integrated suite of software modules that supports the basic internal business processes of a company. ERP gives a company an integrated real-time view of its core business processes such as production, order processing, and inventory management, tied together by ERP applications software and a common database maintained by a database management systems. Free ERP systems, as Free Software Fundation define, it mean share the final binaries and the code

So, the MPS free-Software , is a complete Enterprise systerm for small companies and even medium companies who wants to save money in a Erp, it is free but with the Quality of the commercial product, and having the source code disponible for no extra costs.

The Objective is to have a complete ERP system to share with the open source communitie.
